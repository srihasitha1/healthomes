About Us

Who We Are

We are a purpose-driven team of doctors, engineers, researchers, and strategists, united by a single goal: to make preventive healthcare the first step in everyone’s health journey. Healthomes is led by Dr. Aparna Srikantam — a distinguished medical doctor, researcher and academician with several years of experience in research that focused on finding viable solutions for various healthcare related issues. With her vision at the helm and the overall leadership team — consisting of two experienced clinicians and a tech-business strategist — ensures a balance of medical integrity and digital innovation. With its cumulative experience and expertise of the leadership team, Healthomes envisions endless possibilities in making several homes healthy and happy through building effective customised preventive healthcare solutions for each of their clients.

What we do

At Healthomes, we design digital-first, human-centered health solutions that detect risks, recommend lifestyle changes, and offer smart health predictions tailored to each person.

Digital Preventive Health Assessments

Intelligent tools that help users evaluate health risks based on data, lifestyle, and family history.

Personalized Health Dashboards

Simple, intuitive interfaces that display key health indicators and trends — helping users track their wellness journey.

AI-Powered Risk Prediction Models

Our backend algorithms work silently to flag warning signs before symptoms show up, nudging users to act early.

At-Home Health Monitoring Tools

Easy-to-use digital kits and interfaces that allow self-assessments from home — no hospital visit required.

Corporate & Community Wellness

Custom-built programs that help companies and institutions foster a healthier, more productive population. We believe in data with dignity — your health insights are yours to control, understand, and act upon.

How We Work

We integrate the best of healthcare, behavioral science, and technology. Every feature, every product, every tool we build is shaped by three principles:

  • Human-Centered Design – Easy to use, built with empathy.
  • Evidence-Based Insights – Backed by real health data and expert validation.
  • Technology with Purpose – From AI models to dashboards, we use tech to empower, not overwhelm.

At Healthomes, we don’t just design tools — we design trust. Our work happens across multiple teams:

  • Technology: Developers and engineers building scalable, secure digital platforms.
  • R&D: Medical researchers and data scientists designing intelligent health models.
  • Marketing & Outreach: Educating and engaging users about the power of prevention.
  • Product Development: Shaping user journeys and refining the experience based on real feedback.
