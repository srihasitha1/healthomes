Founded in 2024 by an expert healthcare leader and a visionary biomedical researcher, Dr. Aparna Srikantam, Healthomes is a next-generation med-tech company committed to transforming the way people think about health — from reactive treatment to proactive, preventive care.

In a world where chronic illnesses are on the rise and diagnoses often come too late, Healthomes aims to bring healthcare to the doorstep — making prevention accessible, data-backed, and deeply personal. We leverage cutting-edge technology to detect early signs of health issues, empower individuals with knowledge, and guide families toward healthier living — all from the comfort of their homes.

Because we believe: “The best cure is early awareness.”

Why Us?


In a competitive med-tech landscape, Healthomes is unique because of its:

  • Strong Clinical Backbone – With doctors as founders, care is always our top priority.

  • Deep Tech Ambitions – We’re not here to build apps — we’re here to build impact.

  • Commitment to Prevention – While others treat, we pre-empt.

  • Accessible Innovation – No jargon, no clutter — just clean, honest health guidance.

Our Belief

“Health doesn’t begin in a hospital. It begins at home.”
Healthomes is here to make that belief a reality – with empathy, intelligence, and innovation at every touchpoint.
